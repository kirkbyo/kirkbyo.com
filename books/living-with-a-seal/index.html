Interesting endurance workouts

  • Nickels and Dimes: 5 pull-ups and then 10 push-ups, every minute on the minute.

  • Routine #1

    1. Dumbbell bench press: 15 reps at 35 pounds, 12 reps at 40 pounds, 10 reps at 45 pounds, 8 reps at 50 pounds, and then 6 reps at 55 pounds.
    2. Seated rows (medium weight) 15-12-10-8-6
    3. Military Press: 4 sets of 10 (medium weight).
    4. Triceps pull down: 15-12-10
    5. Curls: 15-12-10 (25 pound dumbbells)
    6. Sit ups: 50... one minute rest... then 50 flutter kicks... 50 four-count flutter kicks (do a flutter kick for 4 seconds, that equals 1).
